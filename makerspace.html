maker.space - the HMA's great all-round DIY workshop.

The Makerspace of the Mannheim University of Applied Sciences is operated by inno.space. Students, staff and professors of all faculties are warmly welcome to bring their projects and ideas to life here and to make use of the wide range of equipment on offer. Further information, our mastering machinery courses which are mandatory for usage of some machines and more can be found in moodle at maker.space.
